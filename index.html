Key empirical results
  • μ invariance confirmed for 9/10 languages (CV < 7%)
  • μ–H correlation: r = −0.521 (alphabetical) vs r = −0.054 (IPA)
  • P₀ = μ × σ ≈ constant across typologically diverse languages
  • Diachronic stability: Δμ ≈ −1.86% over 450 years of French literature
  • Quran η ≈ 20.1% vs Classical Arabic η ≈ 17.5%
